Automated Resume Relevance Scoring

ABOUT THE PROJECT

An automation system designed to evaluate resumes against job descriptions using structured logic, reducing recruiter effort and improving screening consistency.

Scoring & Decision Engine

The system parses resumes, extracts relevant skills and experience, and scores candidates based on alignment with role requirements.

Automation logic ensures consistent evaluation across candidates, eliminating bias introduced by manual screening.

The workflow is extensible and supports rapid iteration.
