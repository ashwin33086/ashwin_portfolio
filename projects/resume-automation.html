Automated Resume Relevance Scoring

ABOUT THE PROJECT

An automation system that evaluates resumes against job descriptions using structured logic, improving screening consistency and reducing recruiter effort.

Scoring & Decision Engine

Resumes are parsed, skills and experience are extracted, and candidates are scored based on alignment with role requirements.

Automated evaluation removes bias introduced by manual screening while maintaining consistent decision logic across candidates.

The workflow is modular and supports rapid iteration as hiring criteria evolve.
