Mood-Based Food Recommendation

About the Case Study

A product case study exploring how food delivery platforms can personalize discovery using real-time mood inputs. The feature maps emotional states to cuisine preferences, contextual triggers, and behavioral patterns to improve relevance and reduce decision fatigue.

Problem & Strategic Insight

Users often open food delivery apps without a clear intent, leading to friction in discovery and longer decision cycles. This solution reframes browsing as a mood-driven journey, aligning recommendations with user context to increase engagement, conversion probability, and long-term retention.
