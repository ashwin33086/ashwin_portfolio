Dynamic Pricing Sensitivity & LTV Optimization

About the Project

This project explores how pricing decisions influence user behavior and long-term lifetime value. It focuses on modeling price elasticity, running controlled pricing experiments, and preventing short-term revenue actions that negatively impact retention and LTV.

Why This Matters

Many pricing systems optimize for immediate revenue at the cost of long-term value. This framework emphasizes sustainable pricing strategies that balance growth, user trust, and business longevity.
