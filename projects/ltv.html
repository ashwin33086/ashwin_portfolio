Dynamic Pricing Sensitivity & LTV Optimization

ABOUT THE PROJECT

This project explores how pricing decisions impact long-term customer lifetime value rather than short-term revenue. It models user elasticity, price shock risk, and behavioral decay to guide sustainable pricing strategies.

Pricing Decision Engine

The pricing engine evaluates user sensitivity across cohorts to determine where price changes drive value versus churn. It integrates elasticity modeling, behavioral signals, and historical response curves to prevent revenue actions that silently destroy long-term LTV.

Rather than optimizing for immediate conversion lifts, the system prioritizes price stability and perceived fairness across the user lifecycle. Experiments are gated through risk thresholds to avoid irreversible trust loss.

By connecting pricing decisions to downstream retention and revenue durability, the framework shifts monetization from reactive tactics to a controlled, compounding growth strategy.
