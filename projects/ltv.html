BRAINSTORMING MODE

Dynamic Pricing Sensitivity & LTV Optimization

About the Project

This project explores how pricing decisions influence user behavior and long-term lifetime value. It focuses on modeling price elasticity, controlled experimentation, and preventing short-term revenue actions that degrade retention and LTV.

Why This Matters

Most pricing systems optimize for immediate revenue without accounting for long-term customer impact. This approach reframes pricing as a strategic decision system rather than a revenue lever.

How the System Thinks

Instead of reacting to short-term revenue signals, the system evaluates user cohorts, elasticity thresholds, and downstream retention impact before recommending pricing actions.
