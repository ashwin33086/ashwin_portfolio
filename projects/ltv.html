BRAINSTORMING MODE

Dynamic Pricing Sensitivity & LTV Optimization

About the Project

This project explores how pricing decisions influence user behavior and long-term lifetime value. It treats pricing as a strategic decision system rather than a short-term revenue lever.

How the System Thinks

Instead of reacting to revenue spikes, the system evaluates elasticity bands, cohort behavior, and downstream retention impact before recommending pricing actions. The focus is on long-term value preservation.

Why This Matters

Short-term pricing optimizations often destroy trust and retention. This framework ensures pricing decisions align with sustainable growth and customer lifetime value.
