Dynamic Pricing Sensitivity & LTV Optimization

ABOUT THE PROJECT

This project analyzes how pricing decisions impact long-term customer lifetime value by modeling elasticity, price shock risk, and behavioral decay.

Pricing Decision Engine

Cohort-level elasticity modeling prevents revenue actions that silently destroy long-term LTV by correlating price moves with retention and behavioral response.

The system prioritizes fairness and trust by gating experiments through risk thresholds instead of chasing short-term lifts.

Monetization becomes a controlled, compounding growth strategy rather than a reactive tactic.
