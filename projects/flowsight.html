FlowSight Analytics — Funnel Automation

FlowSight is an analytics-driven automation system that tracks user funnels, identifies friction points, and converts behavioral data into actionable product and growth decisions.

Decision Intelligence in Action

FlowSight does not just visualize funnels — it interprets them. The system highlights where users drop, correlates events across stages, and enables teams to prioritize fixes based on impact, not intuition.
