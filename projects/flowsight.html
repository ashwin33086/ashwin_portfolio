Decision Intelligence Layer

FlowSight embeds decision logic directly into analytics pipelines, correlating funnel behavior with downstream impact and priority signals so teams act with clarity instead of debate.

The architecture is modular by design. New events, experiments, and cohorts can be introduced without breaking existing logic, ensuring scalability as product complexity increases.

By aligning analytics with execution, FlowSight reduces cognitive load, eliminates metric ambiguity, and accelerates decision velocity across product, growth, and operations teams.
