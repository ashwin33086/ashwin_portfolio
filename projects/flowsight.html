Dynamic Pricing Sensitivity & LTV Optimization

This project explores how pricing decisions impact long-term customer lifetime value rather than short-term revenue. It models elasticity, behavioral decay, and risk thresholds to guide sustainable pricing.

Pricing Decision Engine

The engine evaluates user sensitivity across cohorts to determine where pricing drives durable value versus silent churn, ensuring monetization decisions protect long-term trust and retention.

Pricing experiments are gated through risk thresholds to prevent irreversible value loss. The framework aligns revenue optimization with downstream retention and lifetime value.
