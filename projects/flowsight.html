FlowSight Analytics — Funnel Automation

ABOUT THE PROJECT

FlowSight is an analytics-driven automation system designed to track user funnels, identify friction points, and translate behavioral data into clear product and growth decisions.

The system focuses on reducing guesswork in product prioritization by linking user behavior directly to actionable insights.

Decision Intelligence in Action

FlowSight goes beyond traditional dashboards by embedding decision logic directly into analytics workflows. Instead of presenting static metrics, the system highlights where users drop off, correlates events across funnel stages, and surfaces the highest impact intervention points.

By structuring funnel data into interpretable decision paths, FlowSight enables teams to move faster with confidence. Product, growth, and operations teams can align around the same insights without relying on subjective interpretations of raw data.

The architecture is designed for scalability, allowing new events, cohorts, and experiments to be layered without breaking existing logic. This ensures long-term adaptability as product complexity increases.
