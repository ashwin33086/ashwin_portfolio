ABOUT THE PROJECT

FlowSight is a decision-centric analytics automation platform designed to convert raw funnel behavior into prioritized product actions. It bridges metrics, diagnosis, and execution by embedding reasoning directly into analytics workflows.

Decision Intelligence Layer

The Decision Intelligence Layer forms the core reasoning engine of FlowSight. It transforms fragmented user behavior into structured, prioritized signals that product teams can act on with confidence. Instead of treating analytics as passive dashboards, this layer embeds explicit decision logic that evaluates funnel events in the context of business objectives, user intent, and downstream impact.

The execution layer ensures insights do not remain static observations but are translated into clear, actionable recommendations that map directly to product changes, experiments, or operational interventions. FlowSight continuously monitors funnels across cohorts, devices, channels, and lifecycle stages, allowing teams to distinguish isolated issues from systemic experience failures before allocating resources. Ultimately, the system reframes analytics from a reporting function into a compounding decision asset that grows in value as data, adoption, and organizational complexity increase.
