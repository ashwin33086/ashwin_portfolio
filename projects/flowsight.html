FlowSight Analytics — Funnel Automation

ABOUT THE PROJECT

FlowSight is an analytics-driven automation system designed to track user funnels, identify friction points, and translate behavioral data into structured product decisions.

Decision Intelligence Layer

FlowSight embeds decision logic directly into analytics pipelines, correlating funnel behavior with downstream impact and priority signals.

The architecture is modular by design, allowing experiments, cohorts, and analytics logic to evolve without breaking downstream systems.
